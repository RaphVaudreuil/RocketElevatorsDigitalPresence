Maintenance

A proper preventative maintenance program is required to maintain the equipment in proper and safe operating condition. Periodic, scheduled, preventative maintenance visits by factory trained licensed technicians are required to conduct necessary cleaning, lubricating, adjusting and minor repairs to ensure that your lift or elevator is kept in proper operating condition. Preventative maintenance will extend the life span of your equipment and minimize costly service work not covered by warranty or maintenance.

Our Residential Offers

DuoRocket

The Duo Rocket home Elevator is the perfect alternative to a stair lift or conventional hydraulic elevator. Capable of taking two passengers between floors in under 30 seconds, the Duo Rocket offers freedom and flexibility that cannot be matched.

TrioRocket

The Trio Rocket Home Elevator is our innovative wheelchair elevator and is perfect for the less mobile. Larger than the Duo Rocket Home Elevator but still compact, the Trio Rocket can fit a standard-sized wheelchair and is ideal for users with walking frames.

COD Rocket

The COD Rocket residential home elevator will add a richness to any home. The COD Rocket features one-touch operation, automatic horizontal sliding style doors and offers an array of finishes to compliment any home decor.

Reaf Rocket

The Reaf Rocket is our “Machine Room Less” residential elevator that is operated by a drum-unit. This unit may be installed either at the top or bottom of the hoistway which makes it the perfect option in the absence of a separate machine room or when pit/ overhead space is limited.

Sharingan

The Sharingan is our luxury residential elevator. This model provides all of the benefits of the Panorama plus upgraded features such as, a softer start and stop to enhance overall ride quality and comfort.

Rinnegan

The Rinnegan is our standard residential elevator model which comes with a fine wood veneer cab finishing. This model may be customized to suit personal decor preferences. This elevator has a standard capacity of 1000 lbs., and travels at a speed of up to 36 feet/ minute in both directions.
